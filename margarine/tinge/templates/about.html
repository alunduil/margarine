Margarine is a demonstration application for Unlocked. It showcases the Pillars of Cloudiness (below) while being a fully usable application.

  • Parallel
  • Modular
  • Horizontally Scalable
  • Agile
  • Secure

While being fully functional, this application provides similar (but not a superset of) functionality to that provided by the fantastic services, Readability & Delicious.
